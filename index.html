Welcome to RxTracker

RxTracker leverages the MedicaidSDK to interact with the vast amount of data contained within the Medicaid, FDA, and rxNorm datastores. The SDK is a lightweight, portable, and easy-to-use JavaScript library requiring no downloads or installations.

Get Started

Core Capabilities

Performing realtime drug analytics is easy with RxTracker. Simply select diseases or drugs of interest in order to begin the visualization process. The SDK contains data regarding drug utilization at both the state and national levels, drug pricing over time, and even contains large repositories of social metrics and healthcare quality data.

FDA APIs

RxTracker allows developers to programmatically access the FDA API to gain insight on drug efficacy, history, and purpose.

Medicaid APIs

RxTracker allows for seamless access to the largest Medicaid Datasets including NADAC (National Average Drug and Acquisition Cost) and State Drug Utilization.

RxNorm APIs

Instead of foraging through NDC or IDC codes RxTracker permits users to search for specific drugs based on singular ingredients, disease classes, or generic names.
